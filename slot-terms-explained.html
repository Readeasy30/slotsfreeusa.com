Slot Terms Explained in Plain English

New players often see confusing slot terms. This page explains the most common words without casino jargon.

RTP

RTP means “return to player.” It is a long-term percentage estimate used by game developers. It does not guarantee what happens during a short session.

Paylines

Paylines are the patterns where matching symbols may create a winning combination.

Wild Symbols

Wild symbols can sometimes replace other symbols to help complete combinations.

Scatter Symbols

Scatter symbols may trigger free spins, bonus rounds, or other special features.

Bonus Round

A bonus round is an extra game feature that may include free spins, pick-and-click games, or multipliers.

Free Spins

Free spins are bonus rounds where spins happen without an extra wager during the feature period.

Volatility

Volatility describes how often wins may happen and how large they might be over time. Higher volatility can also mean longer losing streaks.

Important: learning slot terms does not remove risk. Gaming should stay entertainment only.
