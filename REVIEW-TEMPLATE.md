# SlotsFreeUSA Review Template

## Purpose
Use this structure for future social casino and sweepstakes-style review pages.

## Page Structure

### 1. Title
Example:
- Example Social Casino Review
- Example Sweepstakes Platform Review

### 2. Quick Summary
Include:
- mobile experience
- beginner friendliness
- bonus clarity
- support reputation
- safer-play tools

### 3. Important Notice
Explain:
- entertainment only
- no guaranteed winnings
- location restrictions may apply
- users should review terms carefully

### 4. Mobile Experience
Describe:
- speed
- readability
- ease of navigation
- app or browser experience

### 5. Promotions
Explain:
- free spins
- coin systems
- rollover rules
- expiration dates

### 6. Safer-Play Features
Review:
- account limits
- time reminders
- spending controls
- support access

### 7. Final Thoughts
Summarize:
- best fit audience
- strengths
- weaknesses
- beginner friendliness

## Writing Rules
- Keep language simple.
- Avoid hype.
- Never promise winnings.
- Avoid phrases like “winning strategy” or “guaranteed profits.”
- Focus on usability, transparency, and safer play.
