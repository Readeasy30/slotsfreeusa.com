Warning Signs

  • Chasing losses or trying to win money back.
  • Hiding play, spending, or account activity.
  • Borrowing money or using bill money to gamble.
  • Feeling panic, shame, anger, or stress after playing.
  • Ignoring family, work, sleep, or health because of play.

Safer Habits
