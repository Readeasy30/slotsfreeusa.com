Safety first

Responsible Play

Gaming should stay fun. If it causes stress, financial problems, or emotional harm, stop immediately and seek help.

Warning Signs

  • Chasing losses
  • Hiding spending
  • Borrowing money to play
  • Stress or anger after losses

Safer Habits

  • Use time limits
  • Use spending limits
  • Take breaks
  • Play only for entertainment
