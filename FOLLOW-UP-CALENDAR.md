# SlotsFreeUSA Follow-Up Calendar

Last updated: 2026-05-28

This file tracks partner follow-up dates and reply status.

## Rules

1. Record the original contact date.
2. Choose one follow-up date 5 to 7 business days later.
3. Send only one polite follow-up unless the partner replies.
4. If the partner says no, stop outreach for that partner.
5. If the partner says yes, complete the approval checklist before changing public pages.

## Business Day Guide

Business days are Monday through Friday.

Example: if a partner message is sent on Thursday, May 28, 2026, the 5 to 7 business day window is Thursday, June 4, 2026 through Monday, June 8, 2026.
