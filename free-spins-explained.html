FAQ

What are free spins?

Free spins are spins that may be part of a game feature or outside offer. Outside offers can include limits, expiration dates, eligible games, and other rules.

Are free spins always free?

Not always. Some outside offers may require signup, location eligibility, account verification, purchase, deposit, or playthrough rules. Read official terms first.
