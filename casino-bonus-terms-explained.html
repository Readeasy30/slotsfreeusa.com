The terms that matter most

Eligibility

Who can use the offer?

Check age rules, location rules, new-user rules, account rules, and identity verification requirements.

Limits

What limits apply?

Check expiration dates, eligible games, maximum value, redemption rules, and any playthrough or rollover language.

Where do you get help?

FAQ

What are casino bonus terms?

Casino bonus terms are the rules that explain who can use an offer, when it expires, which games count, what activity may be required, and what limits apply.

Why should beginners read bonus terms first?

Beginners should read bonus terms first because offers can include age rules, location rules, expiration dates, eligible games, playthrough rules, and account requirements.
