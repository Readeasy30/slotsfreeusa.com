Green flags

Signs of a clearer site

✅ Rules are easy to find before signup.

Red flags

Slow down when you see this

⚠️ Guaranteed winnings, easy money, or income claims.

⚠️ Missing terms or unclear bonus rules.

⚠️ Pressure to sign up before reading details.

⚠️ No clear support path.

⚠️ Confusing coin, purchase, redemption, or withdrawal language.
