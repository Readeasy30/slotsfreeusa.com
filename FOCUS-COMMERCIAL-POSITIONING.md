# SlotsFreeUSA Commercial Positioning Focus

Last updated: 2026-05-28

## Core Focus

SlotsFreeUSA is a commercial affiliate and media website, not a charity site and not a nonprofit education project.

The site should help adults 21+ find, compare, and understand:

- free slot-style entertainment
- sweepstakes casinos
- social casinos
- Gold Coin and Sweeps Coin systems
- bonus offers and free-spin language
- casino-style prize/reward terms
- partner reviews
- approved affiliate and sponsorship offers

## Business Goal

The goal is to earn revenue through approved:

- CPA affiliate deals
- RevShare affiliate deals
- hybrid CPA/RevShare deals
- sponsorships
- featured partner placements
- display advertising
- future email list monetization after privacy and consent setup

## Language To Use

Use language like:

> SlotsFreeUSA is a commercial affiliate/media site built to send qualified adult U.S. traffic to approved free-slot, social-casino, sweepstakes-casino, and casino-style entertainment partners.

Use language like:

> We are interested in CPA, RevShare, hybrid, sponsorship, featured placement, or display advertising opportunities.

Use language like:

> Partner links go live only after approval, tracking, destination, age/location, offer-term, and disclosure checks are complete.

## Language To Avoid

Avoid making the site sound like:

- a charity
- a nonprofit
- a public-service project
- a classroom education site
- a responsible-gambling organization
- a gambling operator
- a casino
- a prize-redemption platform

Do not lead partner applications with soft wording such as:

> We help adults understand casino terms.

That can be included as a support point, but the lead should be commercial affiliate/media value.

## Prize / Sweepstakes Reminder

The phrase Gerry was looking for is usually:

- sweepstakes casino
- social casino
- Sweeps Coins
- Sweepstakes Coins
- Gold Coins
- prize redemption, subject to operator rules

SlotsFreeUSA may explain these systems and refer visitors to approved partners, but SlotsFreeUSA must not claim to issue coins, run sweepstakes, redeem prizes, process withdrawals, or guarantee winnings.

## Safety Gate

Do not add live affiliate links, tracking links, ad scripts, email capture, payment tools, or public AI until the approval and disclosure gate is complete.
