# SlotsFreeUSA Affiliate Approval Checklist

Last updated: 2026-05-28

Use this checklist after a partner says SlotsFreeUSA is approved or eligible. Approval does not mean links can go live immediately.

No live affiliate links, tracking links, ad scripts, payment setup, private credentials, or unsupported legal claims should be added until this checklist is complete.

## 1. Approval Record

Collect and record:

- Partner name:
- Approved brand name:
- Approval date:
- Approval contact name:
- Approval contact email:
- Affiliate platform or portal:
- Account ID or publisher ID location: do not paste private credentials into the repo
- Approved partnership type: CPA / RevShare / hybrid / sponsorship / display ad / other
- Written approval saved outside the public repo: Yes / No

## 2. Terms To Collect

Before any listing or link goes live, collect:

- Affiliate terms URL or document name
- Program rules
- Allowed traffic sources
- Prohibited traffic sources
- Brand bidding rules
- Email marketing rules
- Social media rules
- Creative and logo rules
- Sub-affiliate rules if any
- Payment terms
- Termination or pause rules

## 3. Age And Location Rules

Confirm and record:

- Minimum age requirement
- Allowed U.S. states or locations
- Restricted U.S. states or locations
- Whether location rules vary by product or offer
- Required age/location disclosure language
- Source of the rule: official partner terms, compliance email, or portal documentation

Do not publish state availability claims unless current official terms support them.

## 4. Offer And Bonus Details

Collect:

- Public offer name
- Bonus amount or promotion language
- Eligible games or products
- Expiration date or campaign period
- Playthrough, rollover, redemption, or coin rules
- Account verification rules
- Deposit, purchase, or no-purchase method language if relevant
- Required terms link
- Any prohibited words or claims

Avoid language such as guaranteed winnings, risk-free gambling, easy money, beat the casino, legal everywhere, or best payout.

## 5. Link And Tracking Review

Before publishing any button or link:

- Final destination URL checked
- Tracking URL checked
- Redirect path checked
- Mobile destination checked
- Landing page brand matches the review
- Landing page displays terms, privacy, and support links
- No private token, API key, account credential, or secret is committed to the repo
- No live tracking script is added unless privacy and consent requirements are reviewed

If a `/go/` redirect path is used later, keep it blocked from search indexing in `robots.txt`.

## 6. Disclosure Requirements

Before a live link appears:

- Disclosure appears near the partner link or offer
- `affiliate-disclosure.html` is linked from the page
- Review page explains whether compensation may affect placement
- CTA text is clear and not misleading
- The page says terms apply when relevant
- The page includes age and location reminders when relevant

## 7. Responsible-Play And Support Checks

Confirm the partner has:

- Responsible-play or responsible-gaming page
- Limit, cooling-off, or self-exclusion information where relevant
- Customer support path
- Terms and conditions
- Privacy policy
- Complaint or help information

If responsible-play or support information is missing, do not publish the live link.

## 8. Review Page Requirements

A live partner review card or page should include:

- Partner name
- Partner type
- Approval status
- Last reviewed date
- Age/location note
- Offer summary
- Terms reminder
- Safer-play note
- Disclosure note
- Final CTA only after review

## 9. Files To Update After Approval

Update these files before live links go public:

- `AFFILIATE-APPLICATION-TRACKER.md`
- `AFFILIATE-SUBMISSION-LOG.md`
- `PARTNER-CHECKLIST.md`
- `PROJECT-STATUS.md`
- Relevant public review page or future partner review file
- `affiliate-disclosure.html` only if disclosure wording needs expansion
- `privacy.html` only if tracking, cookies, email, ads, or analytics materially change

## 10. Final Pre-Live Gate

All must be true before links go live:

- Written approval received
- Terms collected
- Age rules confirmed
- Location rules confirmed
- Final URL checked
- Tracking URL checked
- Disclosure placed
- Responsible-play page checked
- No unsupported claims added
- No private credentials committed
- Manual QA completed

If any item is missing, keep the partner marked as approved for content only or hold until verified. Do not add the live link.
