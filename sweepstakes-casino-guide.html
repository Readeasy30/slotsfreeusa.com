What Is a Sweepstakes Casino?

Sweepstakes-style casinos often use virtual coins or promotional systems instead of direct wagering systems.

Different Than Traditional Casinos

Some sweepstakes platforms separate entertainment coins from promotional sweepstakes coins.

Rules Matter

Every platform has different redemption rules, availability rules, and bonus systems.

Location Restrictions

Availability can vary by state and location. Visitors must review local laws and platform restrictions carefully.

Entertainment First

Visitors should always treat these systems as entertainment and not as a reliable source of income.
